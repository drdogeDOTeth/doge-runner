BASICS

JAB COMBO — attack ×3: two jabs into a rising launcher
UPPERCUT — hold UP + attack (anti-air launcher)
LEG SWEEP — hold DOWN + attack on the ground
AIR KICK — attack in mid-air
METEOR SPIKE — hold DOWN + attack in mid-air (sends foes DOWN!)
SMASH PUNCH — heavy (big knockback, slow windup)
SKY SMASH — hold UP + heavy
QUAKE SLAM — hold DOWN + heavy (hits both sides)
DASH ATTACK — heavy while running
Damage % builds up — the higher a fighter's %, the further they FLY. Knock them past the screen edge to take a stock. 3 stocks each!

SPECIAL MOVES (the SPECIAL button — watch the SP pip recharge)

DOGE — WOW BARK: sonic bark wave rips across the stage. Much range.
BITCOIN — HODL SLAM: moons up, then crashes down with a market-quake shockwave.

CUSTOM — AVATAR NOVA: charges up and detonates a radial energy burst.
