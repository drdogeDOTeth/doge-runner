▶ HOW TO PLAY

Three retro zones: Jungle Jaunt (barrel cannons!), Pipe Panic (bricks & pipes), and Emerald Rush (bounce the springs!). Reach the GOAL! Stomp or roll through critters — never touch the spiky ones. Collect 100 pickups for an extra life and grab all four D·O·G·E letters. The star barrel is a checkpoint.
